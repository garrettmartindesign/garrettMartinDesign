Hey Y'all!

Hi there! I'm Garrett, a graphic designer who followed his passion for visual storytelling. From the Louisiana bayous to the Colorado Rockies, my journey began 24 years ago with a camera and a love for capturing the world around me. My early fascination with photography and calligraphy sparked a lifelong interest in design, eventually leading me to a BFA in Graphic Design and a certificate in UX/UI Design. Today, I call the Denver area home and bring a unique blend of creativity and technical skill to my work. Whether I'm designing a captivating design system, capturing a stunning photograph, or collaborating with a team, I'm always striving to create experiences that resonate and inspire. When I'm not designing, you'll probably find me running trails, exploring the mountains, or experimenting with new creative outlets. I'm excited to connect and explore how my skills can help you achieve your goals. Fluent in Team Collaboration, Sarcasm and Design Thinking.
